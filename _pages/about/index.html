About Me

Hi! I’m Yiwen Li, but you can call me Even. I recently graduated from the University of Toronto, earning an Honours Bachelor of Science degree in Statistics and Economics. Currently, I’m a Research Assistant at the University of Hong Kong , working under the supervision of Prof. Boris Babic . My research primarily focuses on developing robust machine learning models for high-dimensional and complex data, especially in scenarios with limited structured information.

During my senior year at the University of Toronto, I engaged in several innovative research projects, primarily in reliable machine learning and its medical applications, under the mentorship of Prof. Boris Babic . I was also privileged to join FORCOLAB , where I explored the regulatory and ethical dimensions of AI in healthcare and is co-supervised by Prof. Shurui Zhou . In the summer of 2023, I received a fellowship from the Data Science Institute at the University of Toronto, serving as a Summer Research Scholar under the supervision of Prof. Jessica Gronsbell . Currently, as a Research Assistant at HKU, I’m playing a key role in developing the new MA program ‘AI, Ethics and Society .’

My industry experiences have been equally rewarding. In the summer of 2022, I interned as a Data Analyst at UnionPay , where I applied quantitative skills to tackle real-world data challenges. Following this, I joined JD.com, Inc . as a Data Analyst Intern, furthering my pursuit of reliable data-driven solutions through statistical and computational methods.
